About Namaste Café

A journey of authentic Indian flavors and traditions

Our Story

Namaste Café was born from a passion to share the rich coffee and tea culture of India with the world. Founded in 2015, we began as a small family-run business with a simple mission: to serve authentic Indian chai, filter coffee, and traditional snacks using recipes passed down through generations.

Every cup we serve carries the essence of Indian hospitality, from our aromatic masala chai blended with hand-picked spices to our south Indian filter coffee brewed to perfection. Our chefs and tea masters bring decades of experience in traditional Indian cooking and beverage preparation.

Our Journey

  • Our Journey

  • 2015: The Founding

    Started as a small family café in Mumbai, driven by passion for authentic Indian chai and traditional snacks, using grandmother's secret spice recipes.

  • 2017: Expanding Our Menu

    Added south Indian filter coffee and regional delicacies like dosa and samosa chaat, becoming the neighborhood's favorite authentic Indian café.

  • 2019: Growing Team

    Expanded our team to include expert chai wallahs and experienced chefs specializing in Indian street food and traditional snacks.


  • Spreading Indian Café Culture

  • 2021: Community Recognition

    Awarded "Best Authentic Chai" by Mumbai Food Critics and recognized for preserving traditional Indian café culture in modern times.

  • 2023: Cultural Ambassador

    Partnered with local cultural centers to host chai appreciation workshops and traditional cooking classes, sharing Indian heritage through food.
