Namaste Café

Founded in 2018 by Deepjyoti Seal, a passionate coffee and chai enthusiast from Mumbai, our café brings the authentic flavors of India to Brooklyn. We started with a simple mission: to share the rich traditions of Indian chai culture and spices with our New York community.

Every cup we serve blends traditional Indian recipes with modern café techniques. From our signature masala chai made with hand-ground spices to our fusion dishes that celebrate both Indian and American flavors, we create a bridge between cultures through food and hospitality.
